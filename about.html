How Does the AI Work?

Our fish recognition system uses a convolutional neural network trained on thousands of fish drawings. The model runs entirely in your browser using ONNX Runtime Web, which means:

  • ‚úì No data leaves your device - your drawings are private until you choose to share
  • ‚úì Instant real-time feedback - the AI analyzes every brush stroke immediately
  • ‚úì Works offline - once the page loads, the AI runs locally in your browser
  • ‚úì Fast and efficient - optimized for all devices, even phones

The AI was originally developed using PyTorch, trained on diverse fish drawings, and exported to ONNX format for web deployment. This cutting-edge technology makes DrawAFish one of the most innovative online drawing tools available!

Our Story

FishTalk.app is based on the open-source project DrawAFish by aldenhallak. Building upon this amazing foundation, we've expanded the platform with additional features, improved UI/UX, and enhanced community tools.

The original project explored the creative possibilities of AI, combining the simple joy of drawing with the power of modern machine learning. What started as an experiment quickly grew into a community, with thousands of artists drawing fish, sharing their creations, and inspiring each other.

Today, FishTalk.app is used by schools, families, and creative individuals worldwide. We're committed to keeping this platform free, open-source, and accessible forever. Check out the original project on GitHub!
