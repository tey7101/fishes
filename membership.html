How do I upgrade my membership?

Click the "Upgrade Now" button on any plan card above. You'll be redirected to our secure payment page powered by Stripe.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your profile page. Your membership will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Stripe: All major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other digital wallets
  • PayPal: PayPal account, PayPal Credit, and linked bank accounts or cards

You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

Will I lose my fish if I downgrade?

No, your existing fish will remain. However, if you have more fish than your new plan allows, you won't be able to create new fish until you delete some or upgrade again.

Is there a discount for annual plans?

Yes! Annual plans offer significant savings compared to monthly billing. Check the pricing above for details.
